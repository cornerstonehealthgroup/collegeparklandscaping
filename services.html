Mulch & Bed Renovation

Starting at $800

Fresh mulch is the fastest way to transform your landscape's appearance. Clean edges, rich color, and weed suppression - it's the foundation of great curb appeal.

Complete Bed Renovation Includes:

  • Removal of old, degraded mulch and weeds
  • Professional bed edging for crisp, defined lines
  • Weed barrier fabric installation (optional)
  • Premium cypress or hardwood mulch (2-3" depth)
  • Optional: Add new plantings for complete transformation

Mulch Options:

Cypress Mulch

Natural brown color, insect-resistant, lasts 1-2 years in Florida heat

Hardwood Mulch

Rich dark color, decomposes to enrich soil, needs annual refresh

Pine Bark

Budget-friendly option with natural appearance

Typical pricing: Small front yard beds (100-200 sq ft): $800-1,200. Large property with multiple beds (400+ sq ft): $1,800-3,000+. Includes labor, materials, and disposal.
