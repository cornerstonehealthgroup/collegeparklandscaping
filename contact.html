Common Questions

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes, always. I'll visit your property, assess what you need, and give you straightforward pricing with no obligation to hire me.

How quickly can you start?

Usually within 1-2 weeks, depending on the season and current schedule. I'll give you an honest timeline when we talk.

Do I need to be home during service?

Nope! Most clients aren't home. As long as I can access your yard, I'll take care of everything and text you when I'm done.

What if I need to skip a week?

No problem. Just let me know and we'll adjust the schedule. I work around your life, not the other way around.

How do I pay?

I accept cash, check, Venmo, Zelle, or card payment. I'll invoice you after each service or set up monthly billing - whatever works best for you.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. I carry general liability insurance and am properly licensed to operate in Orlando.
